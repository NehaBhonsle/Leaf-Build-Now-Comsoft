How do I pick the right plant?

Check for insects by looking at the underside of the leaves. Avoid plants with drought stress, indicated by yellow foliage. Still unsure? let someone from our nursery help you!

What causes yellowing of leaves?

Poor drainage or improper watering. Either too much watering or too little watering of the plants leads to yellowing.In overly wet soil, roots cannot breathe and underwatering leads to drought, has the same effect.

Do indoor plants need sunlight?

Light is one of the most important factors for growing house plants.Most plants need some amount of light in order to survive but, shade-loving plants can easily get bywith indirect light or even artifical light from regular light blubs.

Can indoor plants reduce stress?

YES. It reduces stress and boots your mood. Caring for plants can reduce physiological and physiological stress compared with mental work this is because caring for plants suppress sympathetic nervous system activity.
